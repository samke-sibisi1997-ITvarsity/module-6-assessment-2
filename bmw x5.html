BMW X-5

Bmw X5 is a mid-size luxury crossover, rear-wheel drive SUV produced by BMW. The X5 made its debut in 1999 as the E53 model. It was BMW's first SUV and it also featured all-wheel drive and was available with either manual or automatic transmission.

In 2006, the second generation X5 was launched, known internally as the E70. A facelift of the E53, the E70 also featured the torque-split capable xDrive all-wheel drive system mated to an automatic transmission.
