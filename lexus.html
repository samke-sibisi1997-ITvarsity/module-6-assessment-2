LEXUS

Lexus is the luxury vehicle division of the Japanese automaker Toyota. The Lexus brand is marketed in more than 70 countries and territories worldwide and is Japan's largest-selling make of premium cars.

The 2021 Lexus RX 350 comes in two trims: base and F Sport. Base 18-inch wheels. LED headlights. Leather-wrapped steering wheel. Power-adjustable front seats. Rain-sensing windshield wipers. Keyless entry with push-button start. Dual-zone automatic climate control. Apple CarPlay, Android Auto and Amazon Alexa integration.
